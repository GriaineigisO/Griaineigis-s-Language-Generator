What is Griaineigis's Generator?

Griaineigis's Generator, named after the Gaelic god Oghma's epithet meaning "shinging sage", generates a complete grammar and dictionary for a randomly generated fictional language upon pressing the button below. Each feature is chosen at random, meaning that every single language created with this generator is unique, with no chance of the same language being created twice. To understand how the generator works, click here.

      Measure Words

      Measure words refer to amounts or collections of a noun, with no reference to the specific number. Unlike classifiers, which are assigned based on the shape of the object, measure words are assigned based on the container of the object, or the masses in which they occur e.g "a glass of water", "a bundle of arrows" and such. Unlike with classifiers, a noun can occur with a range of measure words depending on the context e.g " a glass of water" or "a drop of water".

The animate gender encompasses all nouns that refer to living things, as well as some non-living but "dynamic" things such as fire and moving water. Trees and plants are animate, though their produce, seeds and individual parts are inanimate.

Non-living entities are assigned to either gender based on whether the item is thought to be associated more with men or woman, or at random.             Kerbekulo has two grammatical genders which are "active" and "passive". The active refers to a noun which can act as an agent of a transitive verb, while the passive is any noun that can't be an agent, even if the subject of a middle voice verb.
